# v1.154.248 (2026-07-06)

feat(help): bidirectional article cross-links + related-articles section. New help_article_link table (idempotent ensureLinkTable + install.sql) records article-to-article links parsed from /help/article/{slug} references in the body on ingest; relatedArticles() surfaces them in BOTH directions, so a link authored in one article appears as Related on both. The article page renders a Related articles section, and bulk ingest does a second pass so links resolve regardless of ingest order. Every article already has a stable URL at /help/article/{slug}. Verified on dev: cross-links parsed (3 from the new install/onboarding guide), bidirectional (advanced-search shows the onboarding guide though it never links back), and the Related section renders.
