# Feedback Module User Guide

## Overview

The Feedback Module allows you to submit feedback, suggestions, and corrections about archival records. You can also submit general feedback about the system.

## Submitting Feedback

### General Feedback

1. Go to the main menu and click **Feedback**
2. Click **Add General Feedback** in the sidebar
3. Fill in the form:
   - **Subject**: What is your feedback about?
   - **Type**: Choose from General, Error Report, Suggestion, Correction, or Need Assistance
   - **Remarks**: Describe your feedback in detail
   - **Name and Email**: So we can respond to you
4. Click **Submit**

### Feedback on a Record

When viewing any record (archive item, museum object, library book, gallery artwork, or digital asset):

1. Look for the **Item Feedback** button
   - On most records: A green button below the image
   - On Library/Gallery/DAM: In the dropdown menu under "More"
2. Click the button
3. The record name is filled in automatically
4. Choose the feedback type and write your comments
5. Click **Submit**

## Feedback Types

| Type | Use When |
|------|----------|
| **General** | You have a comment or observation |
| **Error Report** | Something is wrong or broken |
| **Suggestion** | You have an idea for improvement |
| **Correction** | Information in a record is incorrect |
| **Need Assistance** | You need help finding or accessing something |

## What Happens Next

- Your feedback is sent to the archive administrators
- They will review it and take appropriate action
- If you provided contact details, they may reach out to you
- You can track the status of feedback if you have an account

## For Administrators

### Viewing Feedback

1. Go to **Admin** → **Feedback** (or navigate to `/feedback`)
2. You will see a list of all feedback submissions

### Filtering Feedback

Use the sidebar filters:
- **All Feedback**: View everything
- **Pending**: New feedback requiring attention
- **Completed**: Feedback that has been addressed

### Managing Feedback

For each feedback item, you can:
- **Edit**: Update details or change status to Completed
- **Delete**: Remove the feedback (use with caution)

### Marking Feedback Complete

1. Click **Edit** on the feedback item
2. Change **Status** to "Completed"
3. Add any admin notes (optional)
4. Click **Save**

## Tips

- Be specific in your remarks to help administrators understand your feedback
- Include your email if you would like a response
- For corrections, mention exactly what needs to be changed and what the correct information should be
