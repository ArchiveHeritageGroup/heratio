> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Export.

## Overview
Generate finding aids from evidence sets (collections) as printable HTML/PDF or CSV.

## Export Formats
- **HTML/PDF**: printable page with table of contents, all ISAD fields, research notes, Print/Save as PDF button
- **CSV**: downloadable spreadsheet with identifier, title, level, repository, scope, extent, access conditions, notes

## Included Fields
Identifier, Title, Level of Description, Repository, Scope and Content, Extent and Medium, Archival History, Arrangement, Access Conditions, Reproduction Conditions, Physical Characteristics, Research Notes.
