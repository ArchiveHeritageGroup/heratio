> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Workspace.

## Overview
The workspace is your personal research hub showing evidence sets, saved searches, notes, bookings, and activity.

## Features
- **Profile Summary** with name, institution, affiliation
- **Status Alerts** for expired or expiring registrations
- **Stats Row** showing evidence sets, items, searches, visits
- **Weekly Activity** chart (last 7 days)
- **3-Column Layout**: Upcoming/Recent Visits, Evidence Sets, Saved Searches + Notes
- **Quick Tips** for research workflow
- **Create Evidence Set** modal

## Evidence Sets
Evidence sets (collections) group archival items for your research. Add items while browsing, generate finding aids, and export as PDF/CSV.
