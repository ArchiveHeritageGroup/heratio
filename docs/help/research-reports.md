> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Reports.

## Overview
Research reports document findings with structured sections based on templates.

## Creating Reports
1. Click **New Report** from the reports list or a project
2. Choose a template (Research Summary, Genealogical, Historical, Source Analysis, Finding Aid, Custom)
3. Enter title, description, and optionally link to a project
4. Sections are auto-created from the template

## Managing Sections
- **Add Section**: single, from template, or multiple at once
- **Edit**: inline editing with save/cancel
- **Move**: up/down arrows to reorder
- **Delete**: with confirmation
- **Comments**: collapsible per-section comments

## Templates
Managed at /research/report-templates. Each template defines sections with type:title format. System templates cannot be deleted.

## Export
Export reports as printable PDF with table of contents.
