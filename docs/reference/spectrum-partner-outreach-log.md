# Spectrum Partner outreach - enquiry sent to Collections Trust

**Status:** Sent 2026-07-17. Awaiting reply.

An enquiry email was sent to Collections Trust to open a **Spectrum Partner** commercial-licence conversation for Heratio. This is the first outreach on the Spectrum compliance gap recorded in `spectrum-partner-licensing.md`.

- **Sent by:** Johan directly (not via the assistant)

## What was asked

1. The process to become a Spectrum Partner and what Collections Trust needs to assess the application.
2. Commercial licence terms and fee for a product that supports the Spectrum procedures (fee is unpublished / negotiated).
3. What is required for Heratio to be described as "Spectrum compliant" and listed in the Collections Trust software directory.
4. Confirmation that the Partner licence and directory listing apply globally, given Heratio's international market (Southern Africa, wider SADC, Europe, Asia-Pacific).

## Why it was sent

Heratio's `ahg-spectrum` package already implements the Spectrum 5.1 procedures commercially. The Spectrum non-commercial licence forbids commercially competing products and derivative works without prior written consent, so a Partner commercial licence is needed to close a genuine compliance gap - not just a marketing badge. Background: `spectrum-partner-licensing.md`.

Await Collections Trust reply, then work through licence terms, fee, "Spectrum compliant" recognition requirements, and international-scope confirmation. Update this log when a response arrives.
