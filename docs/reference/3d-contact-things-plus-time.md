# Contact: Things + Time (3D archival group, Montreal)

**Why this is here:** a 3D / cultural-heritage group we want to reach out to — a potential collaborator/partner for Heratio's 3D digitisation, exhibition digital-twin, and IIIF-3D work. Logged as a contact-to-make.

## Who they are
**Things + Time** (`https://thingsplustime.net/`) — a Montreal, Canada collaborative platform for **3D archival, multimedia storytelling, and cultural preservation**, framed as "Digital Memory-Keeping" for artists, communities, and institutions.

## What they do
- 3D scanning and multimedia archiving
- Bespoke interactive micro-sites (per-object/per-collection)
- Training and cultural mediation
- Remote education and support

## Stance / philosophy
Emphasise **"critical archiving"** — reflecting on and deconstructing power dynamics in record-keeping, toward anti-oppressive practice. (Aligns with Heratio's ICIP / cultural-sensitivity and community-access themes.)

## Status (as of 2026-06-08)
Early **alpha / limited access** — pre-registration + newsletter on the homepage. Example archival entry: "Bonswa" by Favielle Petit Clair.

## Supporters
Conseil des arts de Montréal, City of Montréal, Conseil des arts du Canada.
