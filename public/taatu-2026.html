The 30-second version

One system, the whole lifecycle

Understand

Research portal, reproductions & bookings, ODRL rights, public access.

Demo 1 live

From a box of scans → a described, searchable record

  • Drop a folder of TIFFs → ingest stages them, virus-scans, makes derivatives.
  • HTR reads the handwriting; NER pulls people, places, dates into the description.
  • Indexed in Elasticsearch - findable in seconds, with IIIF zoom on the page.

The archivist stays in control - AI proposes, the human approves, and every inference is logged for provenance.

Demo 2 live

Walk through the collection - in 3D

  • 3D objects (GLB) sit beside the archival description, Draco-compressed so they load fast.

Demo 3 live

Context that machines can follow

  • RiC turns the fonds into a graph - records, agents, activities, places - with real rico:* relationships.
  • Researchers self-serve: search, request reproductions, book a seat, all rights-aware via ODRL policies.
  • Same core, pluggable per-market compliance (POPIA, GDPR, GRAP, …) - jurisdiction-neutral by design.
