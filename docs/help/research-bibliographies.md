> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Bibliography.

## Overview
Manage bibliographic references with structured entries, import/export, and citation styles.

## Features
- **2-column layout**: entries list + about/actions sidebar
- **Add entries** with title, authors, year, publication, volume, pages, DOI, URL, type, notes
- **Edit/Delete** entries with modal forms
- **Edit bibliography** name, description, citation style
- **Import** BibTeX or RIS (file upload or paste)
- **Export** as RIS, BibTeX, or CSV
- **Citation styles**: Chicago, APA, MLA, Harvard, IEEE, Vancouver
