# v1.154.389 (2026-07-21)

feat(archaeology): #1422 - ahg-archaeology collections management for archaeological sites and finds, generalised from the NMMZ Zimbabwe module. NMMZ modelled the same domain but hard-coded Zimbabwean administrative geography and stored period/material/method as free-text varchars; both nmmz_archaeological_site and nmmz_antiquity are empty on dev and prod, so this is a clean general module rather than a migration, and ahg-nmmz stays as the Zimbabwe regulatory layer (export permits, monument inspections, heritage impact assessments are statutory instruments, not general archaeology). Two new tables, archaeology_site (32 cols) and archaeology_object (33 cols), both extending information_object on the library_item pattern - titles stay on information_object_i18n and no base AtoM table is created or altered. Three corrections to the NMMZ model: (1) archaeology_object.site_id foreign key, since NMMZ recorded only a find_location string with no relationship, so a site's assemblage could never be listed; (2) item_count, so a bulk assemblage such as '312 potsherds from context 4' is one record rather than 312, which is how excavated material is actually catalogued; (3) typology bound to taxonomy terms rather than varchars. Terms rather than a private lookup table because term_protocol attaches ICIP/TK Labels to a term - so 'Human remains', 'Burial site' and 'Rock art site' can be gated by TermProtocolGate across display, OAI, RiC, export and portable bundles - plus getty_vocabulary_link reconciles to AAT and term_i18n translates into the SA languages. New ahg:archaeology-seed-vocabularies command creates 8 vocabularies / 162 terms (46 materials, 43 object types, 18 site types, 13 recovery and 13 dating methods, 11 periods, condition, protection status) pitched at southern African archaeology; idempotent, never edits an existing term, and flags the three terms needing community protocol review without applying a label, since that is a community decision. UI: dashboard with period/material/site-type breakdowns and an unlinked-finds warning, filterable site and find browses, and detail pages showing the site assemblage grouped by material with record and item counts. Routes register via callAfterResolving('router') in register() because /archaeology is a single top-level segment and must beat the locked /{slug} catch-all - loadRoutesFrom in boot() is too late and 404s the dashboard while its child routes resolve (same approach as ahg-articles). Registered via PSR-4 autoload plus bootstrap/providers.php rather than a composer require, so composer.lock is untouched. Verified on dev: migration applied, 162 terms seeded and idempotent on re-run, all five views render against live data, and a Wits-shaped test record (rock shelter, Later Stone Age, C14, 312 glass beads + 47 rim sherds + 1 quartz flake) reported 3 find records / 360 physical items with a correct per-material assemblage. Collections management only; excavation recording (contexts, day books, spatial geometry, calibrated C14) is deliberately out of scope
