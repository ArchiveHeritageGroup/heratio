# v1.154.293 (2026-07-12)

security(xss): replace the bypassable regex HTML sanitiser with a DOM-based allow-list purifier (HtmlSanitizer::clean) - the old strip_tags + regex could not see through HTML-entity encoding, so href='javascript&colon;...' / jav&#97;script: survived strip_tags and executed on click. The new parser decodes entities before a scheme check (http/https/mailto/tel + relative only), drops every attribute not on a per-tag allow-list (kills on* handlers and style), and removes script/iframe/svg/object/etc. ResearchService::sanitizeHtml now delegates to it, so actor history, IO scope_and_content and research annotations/reports/journal share one policy. ext-dom only, no new dependency; verified against 12 bypass payloads + 10 legit-content cases, idempotent
