> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Notifications.

## Overview
Manage research notifications with filters, preferences, and digest settings.

## Tabs
- **All**: all notifications
- **Unread**: with count badge
- **By Type**: filter by alert, invitation, comment, reply, system, reminder, collaboration, booking
- **Preferences**: configure per-type settings

## Preferences
For each notification type, configure:
- In-App (on/off)
- Email (on/off)
- Digest Frequency (immediate/hourly/daily/weekly/never)
