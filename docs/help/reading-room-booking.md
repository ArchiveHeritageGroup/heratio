> Heratio Help Center article. Category: Research / Bookings.

## Overview
Book reading room visits with room selection, date/time, purpose, and material requests.

## Booking Form
- **Select Room**: radio buttons with capacity and equipment info
- **Date & Time**: date picker, start/end time
- **Purpose**: required text description
- **Material Requests**: TomSelect multi-search to pre-request archival items

## Sidebar Info
- Your Information (name, email, institution)
- Information notices (confirmation required, bring ID, 24h cancellation)

## After Booking
Bookings appear in your workspace under Upcoming Visits. Status: pending → confirmed → completed.
