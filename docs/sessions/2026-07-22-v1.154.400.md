# v1.154.400 (2026-07-22)

fix(exhibition): mipmap + anisotropic filtering on walkthrough tiling textures. Every repeating texture in the 3D walkthrough was built with minFilter=LinearFilter and, in loadTex, generateMipmaps=false, so the wall, marble, grout and grass canvases were sampled with no mip chain and shimmered and moired as the visitor walked - worst on a tiled floor receding into the distance, which is most of the frame in a gallery room. Adds texQ() applying LinearMipmapLinearFilter plus anisotropy capped at 8x or the GPU maximum, wired into loadTex, groutFloorTex, grassTexture, marbleTexture and wallTexture. Measured headless on the tiled marble floor of engelbrecht-repository-twin: +19.7% normalised edge detail in the receding mid-distance, where the aliasing was worst; small reductions close up and on walls are the mip chain removing aliasing rather than detail. No new dependencies and no new imports.
